Honey Garlic Chicken

This delicious Asian meal will has an explosion of flavor. Check out how easy it is to make!

After a busy day, come home and cook up this delicious, easy meal. You only need FOUR ingredients!

Ingredients

Chicken Breast

All-Purpose Flour

Butter and Garlic

The Sauce

Steps

Start by making the sauce

Next cut the chicken and cook it

In the same skillet add some butter

Add the chicken back to the skillet

Remove the chicken and enjoy!
