Honey Garlic Chicken

This delicious Asian meal will has an explosion of flavor. Check out how easy it is to make!

After a busy day, come home and cook up this delicious, easy meal. You only need FOUR ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Butter and Garlic
  • The Sauce

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauce
  2. Next cut the chicken and cook it
  3. In the same skillet add some butter
  4. Add the chicken back to the skillet
  5. Remove the chicken and enjoy!
